# Changelog

All notable changes to London in Dance will be documented in this file.

## [1.1.0] - 2026-06-22

### Added

- New genre categories: Jazz, National Dance
- New performance listings across all venues

### Changed

- Reclassified flamenco, tango, and kathak performances from
  Contemporary to National Dance
- Updated Ballet Icons Gala 2027 title and ticket link

## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-15

### Added

- Initial launch of London in Dance
- Ballet and Contemporary performance listings from London Coliseum,
  Sadler's Wells, and Royal Opera House
- Genre and date filters
- Performance detail modal with booking link
